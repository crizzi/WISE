TREATING CYSTIC FIBROSIS (CF)
  • Chest therapy, which consists of Eric's mother or father clapping his back, stimulates coughing so Eric can clear the mucous in his lungs.
  • Inhaling medications help Eric breathe
  • A special diet with vitamin supplements help Eric manage his digestive problems.
