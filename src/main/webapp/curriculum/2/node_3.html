ERIC'S CASE FILE: SYMPTOMS OF CYSTIC FIBROSIS

Eric's accomplishment gave his lungs an overwhelming challenge. Remember, Eric is living with a disease called cystic fibrosis (CF).

THE SYMPTOMS OF CYSTIC FIBROSIS (CF) 

  • CF is a progressive and often fatal disease of the body's mucous and sweat glands.

  • CF directly affects Eric's respiratory and digestive systems.

  • Sweat is responsible for cooling the body. Eric produces too much sweat and loses excessive amounts of salt. This can cause abnormal heart rhythms.

  • He also produces too much mucous. Mucous lubricates body organs, like the lungs and intestines. It prevents tissues from drying out and protects them from infection. Because mucous is very thick, it accumulates in Eric's intestines and lungs.

  • Without proper treatment of his symptoms, Eric can suffer from malnutrition, poor growth, frequent respiratory infections, breathing difficulties, and eventually permanent lung damage.
