A Simple Inheritance Model

In the probability model, events are either dependent or independent. An example of a dependent event is the weather, which is dependent on many things, such as temperature, barometric pressure, wind speed, etc.


Coin flipping is an example of an independent event--it is random whether the coin will land heads or tails.
