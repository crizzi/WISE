Observable Trait: The Ear Lobe

All inherited traits, are controlled by specific segments of DNA, called genes. You have two copies of each gene, one that is inherited from your mother and the other from your father.

CF is not a trait that we can easily observe by looking at someone. You can't tell just by looking that Eric has CF, right? To understand more about inheritance, let's look at an observable trait.


The shape of your earlobeis an easily observable trait.

  • There are two types of ear lobes?attached and free.
  • You have two copies of the earlobe gene, one that is inherited from your mother and one from your father.
  • The combination of genes that you get from your parents will determine what your earlobes look like!
