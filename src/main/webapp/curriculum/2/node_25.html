The Genotypes of Cystic Fibrosis

Figuring Out the Genotypes of Cystic Fibrosis...


Although it is not easy to observe Cystic Fibrosis with the naked eye, doctors and scientists have several tools that allow them to investigate what is going on inside of the body!

Below are X-rays of lungs from two different patients: one with healthy lungs, and one with cystic fibrosis. Remember, the lungs are one of the major organs that are affected by CF. Do you notice a difference between the lungs of the two patients?


CF causes a buildup of mucous in the lungs, which is visible in the X-ray.
