Welcome to my lab! My name is Ms. Sally Codon, and I will be helping you with the next step in your investigation. Let's start by exploring inherited traits.

All living things inherit characteristics, or traits, from their parents. Inherited traits can be observable or unobservable.


Some examples of observable traits are things like hair color and dimples. Other traits are not as easily observed. For example, a trait like blood type is not easy to see with the naked eye
