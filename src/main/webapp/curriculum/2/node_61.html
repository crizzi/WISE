YOUR ASSIGNMENT FOR NEXT WEEK: Interview your family

Is your brother's earwax dry or wet? Can your mom roll her tongue? Complete this activity before the next class to find out!

  1. Click on this link https://accounts.google.com to open a new browser window. Sign in with your Google user name and password, and leave the window open.

  2. Return to this WISE unit. On the next step, you'll see a spreadsheet. Look for your name in Column A to find the row assigned to you.

  3. For each trait in the colored columns, tally the number of phenotypes you observe among your immediate family. For example, if three of your family members can roll their tongues and one cannot, you should enter 3 and 1 in your row under the "Tongue rolling" columns.

  4. Answer the questions in remaining steps of this activity before next week.

Hints:

  • If you see a blank page on the next step, make sure you're signed in to your Google account (https://accounts.google.com), and that you've kept the window open.

  • If you observe another trait that's not already in the spreadsheet, ask your instructor for permission to use it for this assignment.

  • Make sure to include yourself in your tally!
