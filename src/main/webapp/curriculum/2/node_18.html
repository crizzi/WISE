The Ear Lobe

In the model below, letters 'E' and 'e' represent different forms of the earlobe gene. These different forms of a gene are called alleles.

The combination of alleles that you get from each parent determines the shape of earlobes. This combination of two alleles is called a genotype. The physical appearance (how it looks) of the trait that results from each genotype is called the phenotype.

Drag the alleles over the circles to make different genotypes and see what kind of earlobe phenotype results.
