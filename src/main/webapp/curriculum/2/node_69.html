In this activity, you will produce a baby dragon. You will simulate the way genes are passed on to a baby. The genes are given below: 

  • The dominant allele, A, codes for normal skin color.
  • The recessive allele, a, codes for a defective that cannot make skin pigment, so an aa dragon is an albino with completely white skin.

  • W  has wings                        w  no wings

    L   long neck and tail               l    short neck and tail

    A   normal skin pigment           a   completely white,including no freckles

    C  skin color dark green*          c   skin color light green*

    F  fire breather                      f   not fire breather

    H   has horn on nose               h  hornless

 To start, choose just one allele for each parents. 

What are the possible traits the child could have?
