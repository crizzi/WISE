ERIC'S CASE FILE: PERSONAL BACKGROUND

Name: Eric Weller

Mother: Linda Weller

Father: Larry Weller

Older brother: Michael Welller

While on vacation with his family in Colorado, Eric vowed he wouldn't leave until climbing at least part way up the legendary Longs Peak mountain. After a 13-hour hike, Eric not only fulfilled his vow, but he made it all 14,255 feet to the top!

 

Afterward, Eric said:

"I wasn't positive that I would make it. But I have always known that if I SAY I can't, then I won't be able to. But if I THINK I can, then I WILL!"
