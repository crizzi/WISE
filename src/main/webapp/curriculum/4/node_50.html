Increase fuel economy standards to 40 mpg by 2015
Did you know that the Toyota Corolla and the Volkswagon Beetle (diesel) get over 40 miles per gallon (mpg), while the Cadillac Escalade gets only 17 mpg?*

We currently have the technology for even non-hybrid cars to get 40 mpg. If Congress raised fuel economy standards so that automobile companies make cars that get 40 mpg on average by 2015, U.S. emissions of CO2 would be dramatically reduced.
