Renewable Energy Sources
Renewable energy, such as solar and wind power, are renewable because sources like the sun, wind, and ocean will not run out. They also do not produce carbon dioxide like coal-burning power plants. These technologies transform energy from the sun, wind, or water into usable energy without requiring chemical reactions at all.

It could therefore be possible to produce hydrogen using alternative methods such as the hydrolysis of water:

H2O + renewable energy --> H2 + O2


Hydrogen technology could then provide energy without the byproduct of CO2, and reducing our dependence on foreign oil.
