How can Congress reduce the amount of CO2 that we put into the atmosphere?
The biggest contributors to U.S. CO2 emissions are power plants and automobiles. In this project, you have considered several ways to reduce CO2 emissions:

- Promote adoption of cars powered by ethanol (C2H6O)
- Use hydrogen technology for cars, such as hydrogen combustion or fuel cells
- Promote renewable energy sources such as wind or solar power

Two other options are:
- Improve gas mileage standards for new cars
- Impose a tax on gasoline

You might also be aware of other clean energy technologies. Feel free to consider these options, too.

You will now conduct some Internet research to collect additional evidence on what is the best policy to reduce CO2 emissions.
