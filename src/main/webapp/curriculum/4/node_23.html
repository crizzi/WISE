Hydrogen Combustion
In this investigation, we'll focus on how the combustion of hydrogen produces energy.

Lets take a very close look at the hydrogen combustion reaction. Like hydrocarbons, hydrogen (H2) releases energy when it combines with oxygen to form water:

2H2 + O2 --> 2H2O + Energy
