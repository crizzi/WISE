What Can We Do?
Now that you have learned about the greenhouse effect and how humans are contributing to climate change, you are going to use your knowledge to help make a difference.

In this final activity you are going to research and suggest a way our government can reduce the amount of CO2 released into the atmosphere. You will then compose a letter to your congressperson that supports your view.

Your teacher will discuss how you can make a difference by sending your letter to congress.
