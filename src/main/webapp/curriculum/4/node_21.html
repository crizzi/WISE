Other Sources of Energy
In the last activity, you examined one problem with hydrocarbon combustion to produce energy--excessive production of CO2. We need to find cleaner ways to produce energy and reduce our impact on the global climate.

In this activity, you will:
  • Examine cleaner alternatives to hydrocarbon combustion
  • Understand how chemical reactions play a role in these alternatives.
What other types of chemical reactions release energy? What other reactions could we use to get energy?
