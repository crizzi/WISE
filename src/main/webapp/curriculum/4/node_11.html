Evaluate your explanation
As you work through this project, you will explain what you learn like the last step. Good explanations connect ideas, and these ideas can come from inside or outside of chemistry class. Use the following rubric to evaluate your explanations. The same type of rubric will be used by your teachers to assess your work in the project.

Score

Description
4
Complex links (more than one connection) - all scientific ideas are connected meaningfully
3
 Simple link (one connection) - at least two scientific ideas connected meaningfully
2
 Partial link - mentions at least one scientific idea
1
 Irrelevant - something is written but doesn't address the question asked

How do your answers compare? You can always go back and edit your work, or go back to the simulations. If you have any questions about these guidelines be sure to ask your teacher.
